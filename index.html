Customer Reviews Summary

Customers find this golf set offers good value for money and is particularly suitable for beginners, with well-made clubs that hold up well and are easy to carry.

The set receives positive feedback for its playability, with one customer noting the nice weight on the club heads.

However, the size receives mixed reviews, with some finding the case size perfect while others say the irons are too short.

Moreover, the driver quality and dent resistance are concerns, with multiple customers reporting issues with the driver being unusable and the driver dented after just 6 rounds of play.
